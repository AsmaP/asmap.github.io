About

Asma began what soon became a very successful blog in 2012. After continuing to make an impact as the first US. based Muslim fashion blogger, and working with brands such as Chanel and Gucci, she wanted something more. A few years later she began learning how to code and design beautiful websites. Along with developing social media strategies to help small businesses become successful, she enjoys boxing and eating chocolate in her free time.
