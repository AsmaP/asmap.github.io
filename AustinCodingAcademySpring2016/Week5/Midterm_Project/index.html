Designer Spotlight: Noon by Noor

Noon By Noor is a Bahrain based womenswear label founded in 2008 by designers, and cousins, Shaikha Noor Al Khalifa and Shaikha Haya Al Khalifa. Modern and effortless with a focus on striking prints and intricate embellishments. Inspiration is drawn from both Eastern and Western influences with an emphasis on sharp tailoring, clean silhouettes and attention to detail.
